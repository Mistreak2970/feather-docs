# Moderation (Old)

## Moderation in Feather

To ban or mute someone, click Moderation in the Config UI.

![](/feather-docs/.gitbook/assets/image-10.png)

After clicking Moderation, you should see this UI

<figure><img src="/feather-docs/.gitbook/assets/image-3.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click whatever you want to do.

<figure><img src="/feather-docs/.gitbook/assets/image-4.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once you have click one of them, you should see something like this.



Click "Ban" or "Mute" and you should see something like this:

<figure><img src="/feather-docs/.gitbook/assets/image-5.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click one of the users that you want to ban/mute.



<figure><img src="/feather-docs/.gitbook/assets/image-6.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Enter the reason for the ban/mute. If you want to mute/ban them permanently, leave the time empty.



You have now muted or banned someone!
