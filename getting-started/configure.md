# Configure

## Configure Feather Essentials

Configuring Feather Essentials is easy, just go to the Config UI and click Modules.

<figure><img src="/feather-docs/.gitbook/assets/image-8.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Options

The options that can be configured are:\
\
Chat Ranks: Turn off or on ranks above your name and in Chat

Language: Switch languages between Portuguese, Spanish and English.

/pay: Decide if your players can use the /pay command.

<figure><img src="/feather-docs/.gitbook/assets/image-9.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click "Submit" when you are ready and have selected your modules.

Your changes now should have taken affect - You have now configured Feather!
